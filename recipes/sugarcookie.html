Sugar Cookie Dough Protein Bites

Home

Description

This edible protein sugar cookie dough is inspired by the classic bakery-style treat, with a hint of almond for nostalgic sugar cookie flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup vanilla protein powder
  • 1/4 cup dry non-fat milk powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon stevia, or to taste
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon almond extract, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons water, or as needed
  • 1 tablespoon jimmies (optional)

Steps

  1. Whisk protein powder, milk powder, stevia, and salt together in a bowl. Add butter, vanilla, and almond extract; mix until crumbly.
  2. Add water, 1 teaspoon at a time, until a soft dough forms that is not sticky. Fold in the sprinkles.
  3. Scoop small portions, about 2 teaspoons each, and roll into balls. Place balls on parchment or a plate. Refrigerate until slightly firm, 10 to 15 minutes.
